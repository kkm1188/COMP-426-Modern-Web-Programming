The Death of Achilles

According to legends, Achilles was killed by Paris, who was vengeful after Achilles murdered his brother. Paris ambushed Achilles as he entered Troy and hit him in his heel with a poisoned arrow. Because this was the one part of his body that was mortal, the poisoned arrow killed him. Achilles died on the spot, still undefeated in battle.
